# 🥷 Ninja Hands

Catch flying fruit with your actual hands. Dodge bombs. MediaPipe tracks 21 3D landmarks per hand — close your fist to grab, open to release. Two-hand mode for double the chaos.

▶️ [Play Now](https://ashleywolf.github.io/ninja-hands/)

## How to Play

Open in Chrome and allow webcam access. Hold up your hands in front of the camera. Fruit flies across the screen — close your fist when your hand overlaps a fruit to catch it. Avoid bombs. Stars give bonus points and slow-mo.

## Tech

- MediaPipe HandLandmarker (21 3D keypoints per hand, 2 hands)
- Grab detection via fingertip-to-palm distance
- HTML5 Canvas
- Single file, CDN-loaded

## Input

Webcam (hand tracking, grab/release gestures)
